## 🎨 Photo Selection Made Simple

### Good Photo Checklist:
✅ Sharp and clear (not blurry)  
✅ Well-lit (natural light is best)  
✅ Clean and tidy (no clutter)  
✅ Horizontal orientation (landscape)  
✅ Makes you feel "I want to visit!"  

### Bad Photo Avoid:
❌ Blurry or out of focus  
❌ Too dark  
❌ Messy or cluttered  
❌ Vertical/portrait orientation  
❌ Doesn't look inviting  

**Pro Tip:** Show photo to a friend for 5 seconds, then ask "Would you want to visit this hotel?" If yes = good photo!
